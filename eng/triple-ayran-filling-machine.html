DTriple Ayran Filling Machine

KORU DOUBLE ROTARY CUP FILLING MACHINE WORKING PRINCIPLE





BOARD

Our machine is connected to the motor and reducer and gets its movement from the mechanical transmission and rotates the rotary table on it, the sockets on the rotary table will be designed to fit a single type of glass..

DROP THE CUP

The cups are automatically dropped in groups of four, while the vacuum at the bottom separates the cups and ensures they fit into the slot.

VOLUMETRIC NOZZLE FILLING

When the photocell detects the glass, it fills the desired volumetric product into the glass in one go. (It is suitable for filling ayran and fermented milk.).

PUTTING THE LID ON

Ready-made aluminum lids arranged in fours in the slot are automatically taken by vacuum and placed on the cup.

COVER RELEASE SYSTEM

A custom design will be made based on the sample, consisting of two lid release devices powered by a single piston. Lid suction cups conform to the lid's shape. Suction cups that deform over time can be easily replaced by unscrewing the screws.

LID CONTROL SENSOR

The bowls with lids on pass under the lid control inductive sensor and come under the gluing station..

ADHESIVE

It adheres to the aluminum foil at the desired temperature. Temperature control is adjusted via the display on the panel (THE HEAT CONTROL DEVICE). Magnetic sensors on the adhesive piston prevent potential problems with the machine.

EXIT CONVEYOR

The finished products are lifted onto the conveyor using a lifting piston and a pusher arm, aligning them with the belt. The bowls will be manually collected from the belt by your designated personnel. The conveyor belt system is aligned with retaining brackets. The conveyor belt will be designed with a PU belt system, and non-slip wicks underneath the belt will prevent rapid wear, extend the conveyor's life, and ensure increased use.
