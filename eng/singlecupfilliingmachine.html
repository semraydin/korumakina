About Us

About Us

Our company aims to offer the best solutions to our customers with its commitment to quality products and service. We continue to operate in accordance with our principles of trust, honesty, and customer satisfaction, and we aim to reach a wider audience by constantly evolving.

Our company specializes in the production of ayran and fruit juice filling machines. We offer modern and efficient solutions to the food industry by manufacturing with the principles of high quality, hygiene, and reliability. We continue to serve with innovative technologies, always prioritizing customer satisfaction.

Founded in Sakarya in 1997, KORU MAKİNA is a leading machinery manufacturer offering innovative solutions for the milk, dairy products, and fruit juice processing sectors.

Koru Makina was founded by Necmi Koru. Since 2024, it has been managed by the second generation, his son Kayra Yiğit Koru.
