Ayran Filling Machines

Ayran filling machines are industrial equipment that allow dairy producers, especially those in the food and beverage industry, to fill ayran hygienically, quickly, and in standard quantities into bottles, glasses, or other containers. They are generally available in fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual types.

Fruit Juice Filling Machines

Fruit juice filling machines are equipment that fills fruit juices and similar beverages hygienically, quickly, and accurately into bottles, cartons, or other packaging. They generally operate in two main types: cold filling and hot filling.
